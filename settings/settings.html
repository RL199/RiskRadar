Configure Risk Radar.

Appearance

Scanning

When on, Risk Radar scans every page you visit on its own, without opening the popup: it shows a colour-coded risk badge on the toolbar icon and highlights findings on the page. It runs the same URL, reputation, content, and link checks the popup does; the AI analysis is never run automatically.

Safety warnings

Choose when Risk Radar interrupts a risky navigation with a confirmation prompt. Both are on by default.

AI

Automatic scans call the AI (and bill your provider) every time you open the popup on a scannable page, and need a key for the selected provider. Manual scans only run when you click Analyze.

Used for AI-based content analysis. Stored locally on your device.

Reputation

Optional. Safe Browsing already works without a key via Google's public Transparency Report; a key upgrades it to the official Lookup API. Stored locally on your device.

Needed for the VirusTotal check. With a key the row shows an inline malicious/total verdict; without one the popup shows a "Key needed" prompt. Stored locally on your device.

Content highlights

Choose which content findings get highlighted on the page. Changes apply the next time the popup scans a page.

Link highlights

Choose which links get outlined on the page. Changes apply the next time the popup scans a page.
