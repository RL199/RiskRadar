Appearance

Scanning

When on, Risk Radar scans every page you visit on its own, without opening the popup: it shows a colour-coded risk badge on the toolbar icon and highlights findings on the page. It runs the same URL, reputation, content, and link checks the popup does; the AI analysis is never run automatically.

When on, clicking a link checks the reputation of both the link itself and the address it finally lands on, since redirects can change it along the way. A small overlay on the page shows a verdict for each address.

Open right away never delays your browsing: the site loads while the check runs and the overlay reports the verdicts. The two wait choices hold the click on a Risk Radar checking screen until the verdicts are in: warn lets you choose whether to enter a site that failed the check, while block refuses to open it.

Safety warnings

Applies both when you click a link flagged as malicious and when you type a risky address in the URL bar.

Warn shows a confirmation you can accept to continue anyway. Block cancels the navigation and shows a message that the website is blocked. Do nothing lets the navigation through without interruption; flagged links are still outlined.
